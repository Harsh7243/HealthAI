# 🌟 **HealthAI: Your Future-Ready Healthcare Companion** 🚀

Welcome to **HealthAI** – where advanced AI meets healthcare to deliver a smart, intuitive, and interactive health management experience. From intelligent report analysis to emergency assistance and personalized diet planning, HealthAI empowers you to take control of your well-being.

---

## 🎯 **Project Overview**

HealthAI is an AI-driven healthcare platform designed to simplify complex medical processes into clear, actionable insights. By leveraging advanced language models and multi-agent systems, it provides real-time assistance, medical report interpretation, emergency support, and personalized nutrition guidance.

---

## 💡 **Core Features**

1. **💬 Chat with HealthAI:** Your intelligent, always-available health assistant.  
2. **📝 Report Analyzer Pro:** Instant medical report insights with actionable recommendations.  
3. **🚑 Emergency Care Connect:** Real-time assistance during critical situations.  
4. **🥗 Smart Diet Planner:** Personalized nutrition planning powered by AI.  

---

## 🔍 **Features in Detail**

### 💬 **Chat with HealthAI**
Interact with an AI-powered health assistant for real-time, evidence-based guidance.

- **How It Works:** Users can ask health-related queries, and the system uses Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) to provide accurate and context-aware responses.
- **Agents Involved:**
  - **Chat Assistant:** Handles conversational queries.
  - **Document Processor:** Extracts insights from uploaded reports.
  - **Research Agent:** Fetches up-to-date medical information.

---

### 📝 **Report Analyzer Pro**
Upload medical reports (PDF/TXT) and receive clear, structured insights.

- **How It Works:** NLP techniques extract key findings, highlight risks, and generate actionable recommendations.
- **Agents Involved:**
  - **Document Processor:** Parses and structures report data.
  - **Positive Analyzer:** Identifies healthy indicators.
  - **Negative Analyzer:** Flags potential concerns.
  - **Recommendation Agent:** Suggests improvements and next steps.

---

### 🚑 **Emergency Care Connect**
A reliable digital support system during emergencies.

- **How It Works:** Uses geolocation to identify nearby hospitals and enables quick communication for immediate assistance.
- **Agents Involved:**
  - **Location Agent:** Tracks and shares user location.
  - **Emergency Bot:** Coordinates emergency responses.

---

### 🥗 **Smart Diet Planner**
AI-powered nutrition planning tailored to your lifestyle and preferences.

- **Features:**
  - **📸 Meal Analyzer:** Upload an image of your food to get a detailed breakdown of nutritional content (calories, macros, etc.).
  - **🥘 Ingredient-Based Planning:** Enter available ingredients and receive a personalized, healthy, and tasty diet plan.
  - **📅 Structured Meal Plans:** Generates diet schedules aligned with daily routines.
  - **🍳 Recipe Suggestions:** Provides step-by-step recipes for recommended meals.

- **How It Works:** Combines computer vision and AI-based recommendation systems to analyze food and generate optimized diet plans.

---

## 🛠 **Tech Stack**

- **Frontend:** Streamlit for interactive UI  
- **Backend:** Python with LangChain for multi-agent orchestration  
- **Database:** FAISS for efficient document retrieval  
- **APIs & Tools:** Google Generative AI (embeddings), Geolocation APIs  
- **Visualization:** Plotly for intuitive data representation
